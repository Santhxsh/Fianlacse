    Welcome to Baker's Kitchen

    Baker's Kitchen is renowned for its delicious biscuits, cakes and pastries. Beautiful displays of carefully crafted biscuits. These biscuits can be found on every bakery shop in Lucknow, usually buzzing with locals enjoying a cake or biscuit along with a cup of tea (Chai) or a coffee. Our hand carved biscuit moulds are an example of how traditional hand-craft and baking come together, making ordinary biscuit making into a work of art. Each mould is carefully carved, each one slightly different and with a unique identity of its own..
